Mouse kidney
============

--------------

`|image0| <files/loci/images/software/mouse-kidney.jpg>`_

Mouse kidney section from Invitrogen's `FluoCell prepared slide
#3 <http://products.invitrogen.com/ivgn/product/F24630?CID=Search-Product>`_.
The section is stained with Alexafluor 488 WGA, Alexafluor 568
phalloidin and DAPI, and is imaged as two series: an XYZ stack (25
planes), and an XZY version (46 planes) of the same zone.
