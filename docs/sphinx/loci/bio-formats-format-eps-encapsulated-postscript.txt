EPS (Encapsulated PostScript)
=============================

.eps, .epsi, .ps

Developer: 

`Adobe <http://www.adobe.com/>`_

Support

Export: |yes|

Supported Metadata Fields: 

`EPS Reader <http://dev.loci.wisc.edu/trac/software/wiki/EPSReader>`_
